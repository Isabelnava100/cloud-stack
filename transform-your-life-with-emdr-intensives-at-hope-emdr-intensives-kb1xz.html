At Hope For The Journey, we offer a range of therapeutic services that focus on trauma therapy, high functioning anxiety, and perfectionism. Our team is specifically trained in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) therapy, which is highly effective for addressing trauma and anxiety issues. We also provide EMDR intensives, which are concentrated sessions designed to process trauma more quickly and effectively, allowing clients to make significant progress in a shorter time frame.

EMDR therapy is a structured treatment approach that helps individuals process and integrate traumatic memories. During an EMDR session, clients are guided through a series of eye movements or other forms of bilateral stimulation while focusing on distressing memories. This process helps to rewire the brain's response to trauma, enabling individuals to develop healthier coping mechanisms and reduce the emotional impact of those memories. EMDR has been proven effective for various issues, including high functioning anxiety and trauma.

EMDR intensives are specialized therapeutic sessions designed to immerse clients in the EMDR process over a more extended period, typically within a single day or a few days. These intensives are especially beneficial for those dealing with significant trauma or anxiety, as they allow for deeper work within a condensed timeframe. Our trained therapists at Hope For The Journey tailor each intensive to fit the individual's needs, ensuring a focused and effective therapeutic experience.

Our services are designed to support a broad range of clients, including children, teens, and adults who are experiencing high functioning anxiety, trauma, or perfectionism. Whether you are struggling with anxiety that affects your daily life, processing traumatic experiences, or seeking to overcome perfectionistic tendencies, our skilled therapists can help. We offer a compassionate, understanding approach that empowers clients to navigate their challenges and reach their goals.

How can I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule an appointment with Hope For The Journey by visiting our website and filling out the contact form, or by calling our office directly. We are here to assist you through the booking process and answer any questions you may have. Our team can help you find a convenient time for either in-person or video counseling, depending on your preference and needs.

What can I expect during my first session?
During your first session at Hope For The Journey, you can expect a warm and welcoming environment. Your therapist will take time to understand your background, concerns, and treatment goals. This session is an opportunity for you to share your experiences and for the therapist to explain the therapeutic process. Whether you are seeking help for high functioning anxiety, trauma, or perfectionism, we will collaborate on a plan that suits your needs and sets the foundation for your journey towards healing.
