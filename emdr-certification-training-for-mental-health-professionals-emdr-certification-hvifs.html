Frequently Asked Questions

EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) is a psychotherapy approach designed to alleviate the distress associated with traumatic memories. It is recognized as an effective treatment for post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and other trauma-related symptoms. By facilitating the processing of traumatic experiences, EMDR helps individuals to shift their perceptions and memories, leading to improved emotional well-being. Scaling Up EMDR specializes in providing resources and training for mental health professionals to enhance their understanding and application of EMDR therapy, ensuring practitioners are equipped to deliver effective treatment.

Our training programs are designed to be both comprehensive and accessible, enabling practitioners to integrate EMDR into their therapeutic practices.

The EMDR Certification process at Scaling Up EMDR is structured to validate the competence of practitioners in the application of EMDR therapy. We emphasize the importance of continued education and support to ensure competent practice. Upon successful completion, certified practitioners gain recognition for their expertise in EMDR, enhancing their credibility within the mental health community.
