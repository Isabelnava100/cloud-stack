How can I tell if my sprinkler system needs repairs?
Signs that your sprinkler system may need repairs include uneven watering, water pooling in certain areas, reduced water pressure, or visible leaks. If your system is not providing adequate coverage or if you notice brown patches on your lawn, it may be time for an inspection. Regular maintenance checks can help detect these issues early. Local Sprinkler Pros is equipped to identify and resolve any problems, ensuring your system remains functional and efficient.
