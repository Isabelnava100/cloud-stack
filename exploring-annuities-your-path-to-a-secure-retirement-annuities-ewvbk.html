Frequently Asked Questions

Annuities are financial products designed to provide a steady income stream, typically for retirees. They can be funded through a lump sum payment or a series of payments. The primary aim of annuities is to ensure that individuals have a reliable source of income during their retirement years. They come in various forms, including fixed, variable, and indexed annuities, each with different features and benefits. Lunsford Insurance specializes in helping clients choose the right annuity options as part of comprehensive financial planning, ensuring that their retirement strategies align with their future goals and needs.

Annuities function by converting your money into a future income stream based on your preferences. Initially, you pay a premium—this can be done all at once or through multiple payments. After this accumulation phase, the insurance company pays you back over time, either for a set period or for your lifetime. The payment strategy can be customized according to your financial situation and retirement strategies. At Lunsford Insurance, we work closely with you to develop a personalized approach to annuities, ensuring they fit seamlessly into your overall financial planning efforts.

Annuities offer multiple benefits for retirement planning, primarily the promise of a fixed income that helps manage the risk of outliving your savings. They can be tailored to fit your cash flow needs and can include options for growth based on investment performance. Additionally, they can provide tax-deferred growth, meaning you won’t pay taxes on earnings until they're withdrawn, making them a strategic choice within a retirement portfolio. At Lunsford Insurance, we emphasize the importance of integrating annuities into your overall retirement strategy to enable a secure and comfortable retirement.

When rolling over a 401K, it's crucial to evaluate several factors, including fees, investment options, and the overall financial goals you aim to achieve in retirement. You have various options: you can roll over into an IRA, another 401K, or even purchase annuities. Each choice carries distinct features that may benefit your financial planning differently. At Lunsford Insurance, we guide you through the rollover process, helping you choose the option that aligns best with your retirement strategy, thus ensuring optimal growth and protection of your assets.
