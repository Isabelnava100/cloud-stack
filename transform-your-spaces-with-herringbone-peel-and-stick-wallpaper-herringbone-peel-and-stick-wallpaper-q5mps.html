Peel & stick wallpaper is a type of removable wallpaper that comes with an adhesive backing, allowing you to easily peel it off a backing paper and stick it directly onto the wall. This makes it incredibly user-friendly, especially for renters or those who want to change their decor frequently. The material can be repositioned during installation and removed cleanly, leaving no sticky residue behind. Musewallstudio specializes in creating high-quality peel & stick wallpaper that lasts indefinitely, enriching your space with beautiful patterns like herringbone.

Applying Musewallstudio's peel & stick wallpaper is a straightforward process. First, you ensure your walls are clean and dry. Then, simply measure and cut the wallpaper to fit your wall space. Peel off the backing and start at the top or corner of the wall, pressing it into place as you go. Smooth out any bubbles or creases with a squeegee or your hand. If you need to reposition it, simply peel it away and try again. This easy application is one of the reasons our customers rave about our wallpaper!
