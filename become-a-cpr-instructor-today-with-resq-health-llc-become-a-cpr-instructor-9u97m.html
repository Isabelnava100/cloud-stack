What is the importance of CPR training in today's society?
In today's society, CPR training is vital as it equips individuals with the ability to respond to life-threatening emergencies such as cardiac arrest effectively. Immediate CPR can significantly increase the chances of survival until professional help arrives. By promoting CPR training through our programs, ResQ Health aims to foster a culture of preparedness and responsibility, empowering everyday citizens to take action during critical moments. This effort not only saves lives but also creates a community that values health and safety.
