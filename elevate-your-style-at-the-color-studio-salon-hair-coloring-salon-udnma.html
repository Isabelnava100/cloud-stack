Balayage is a hair coloring technique that involves painting color onto the hair in a way that creates a natural, sun-kissed effect. It differs from traditional highlights, which use foils to separate and lighten sections of hair. Balayage delivers a softer, more blended look with less maintenance, making it an excellent choice for those who want dimension and movement without the commitment of regular touch-ups. At our North Dallas hair coloring salon, our team of experts is skilled in this technique to provide you with stunning results.
