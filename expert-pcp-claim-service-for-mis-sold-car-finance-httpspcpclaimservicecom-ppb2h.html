Frequently Asked Questions

PCP Claim Service is a specialized company that helps individuals file financial compensation claims for mis-sold PCP car finance. If you've entered into a Personal Contract Purchase (PCP) agreement and believe it was mis-sold to you—whether due to misleading information, inappropriate advice, or failure to explain key aspects of the agreement—our dedicated team can assist you in pursuing the compensation you deserve.

Mis-sold PCP car finance refers to situations where customers were provided with inadequate or misleading information regarding their PCP agreements. This can include not being informed about the total cost of the finance, being led to believe that ownership of the vehicle would revert to them when it actually would not, or being sold a plan that did not fit their financial circumstances. If you feel you were misled when acquiring your vehicle, you may be eligible for a financial compensation claim.

The claim process with PCP Claim Service is straightforward. First, you need to contact us through our website, www.pcpclaimservice.com, and submit your initial details. Our team will evaluate your situation and determine if you have a valid claim. If so, we'll guide you through the documentation needed and assist you in submitting your claim to your finance provider. Throughout the process, we keep you informed and support you until your case is resolved.

To initiate your financial compensation claim with PCP Claim Service, you will need to provide key information including your personal details, details of the PCP agreement, and any correspondence you have had with your finance provider. It's also helpful to include information on why you believe the finance was mis-sold to you. The more details you can provide, the better we can assess your situation and file a robust claim on your behalf.

The duration of the compensation claim process can vary depending on the complexity of your case and the responsiveness of the involved finance companies. Generally, our clients often see resolutions within a few weeks to several months. At PCP Claim Service, we work diligently to expedite your claim while ensuring that all necessary documentation is provided to maximize your chances of a successful outcome.

Am I eligible to claim for mis-sold PCP car finance?
Eligibility for claiming mis-sold PCP car finance typically depends on whether you can prove that misleading information was provided at the time of sale, or that the contract was unsuitable for your financial needs. If you were not clearly informed about the terms of your PCP agreement or felt pressured to take a car finance deal that didn't meet your needs, you may be entitled to obtain compensation. A consultation with our team at PCP Claim Service can help clarify your eligibility.

How much compensation can I expect to receive?
The amount of compensation you may receive for a mis-sold PCP car finance claim depends on various factors, including the nature of the misrepresentation and the financial impact it had on you. Compensation can cover the difference between what you expected to pay and what you actually paid, along with any additional losses you may have incurred due to the mis-selling. At PCP Claim Service, we work to ensure you receive the maximum compensation available for your circumstances.
