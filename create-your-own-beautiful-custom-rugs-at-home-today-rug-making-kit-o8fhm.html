A rug tufting kit works by allowing you to create a rug through a technique called tufting. To begin, you stretch the canvas backing on a frame and use the tufting gun to stitch yarn into the fabric. The gun punctures the canvas and loops the yarn, creating your desired design. After tufting, you apply a backing to secure the yarn and finish your rug. How do I maintain and clean my custom rugs?
Maintaining and cleaning your custom rugs is essential for their longevity and appearance. Regularly vacuum your rug to remove dust and debris. For stains, gently blot the area with a clean cloth and warm, soapy water; avoid scrubbing, as this can damage the fibers. If your rug requires a deeper clean, consider professional cleaning services that specialize in handmade rugs. Ensuring proper care will keep your custom rug looking vibrant and new for years to come.
