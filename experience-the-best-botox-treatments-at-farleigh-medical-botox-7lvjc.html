Botox is a popular cosmetic treatment derived from the Botulinum toxin. It works by temporarily paralyzing specific muscles to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. At Farleigh Medical, our aesthetics clinic offers a range of Botox treatments designed to enhance your facial rejuvenation. Botox has gained popularity due to its effectiveness in minimizing signs of aging and providing a smoother, more youthful complexion.

Botox works by blocking signals from the nerves to the muscles. When injected into the targeted muscle, it prevents those muscles from contracting, leading to a reduction in the appearance of wrinkles. This effect typically lasts three to six months, after which repeat Botox treatments may be necessary to maintain the desired results. Are there any side effects associated with Botox?
As with any medical treatment, Botox can have side effects, although they are generally mild. Common side effects include temporary bruising, swelling, or redness at the injection site. In rare cases, patients may experience headaches or drooping eyelids. At Farleigh Medical, our experienced team takes precautions to minimize risks and ensures you understand any potential side effects before proceeding with Botox treatments.

How long do the results of Botox last?
The results of Botox treatments typically last between three to six months, depending on factors such as the area treated and individual metabolism. Regular follow-up sessions at Farleigh Medical can help maintain your desired look. We recommend scheduling touch-up appointments as needed to keep your skin looking youthful and vibrant, ensuring ongoing facial rejuvenation that meets your aesthetic goals.
