Frequently Asked Questions

Print-On-Demand (POD) is an e-commerce business model where products are printed only after a customer places an order. This means there's no need to hold inventory, reducing risk and upfront costs. Entrepreneurs create designs for various products, such as t-shirts, mugs, and phone cases, and upload them to a POD platform. When a customer orders, the product is printed and shipped directly to them, allowing for scalability and customization without financial strain.
