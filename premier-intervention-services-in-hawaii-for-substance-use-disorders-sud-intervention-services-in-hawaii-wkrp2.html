What is dual diagnosis treatment, and how do you provide it?
Dual diagnosis treatment at Exclusive Hawaii Rehab addresses both addiction and any co-occurring mental health disorders, such as anxiety or depression. Our licensed therapists and medical professionals conduct comprehensive evaluations to create personalized treatment plans. By utilizing a variety of therapeutic strategies, including counseling, medication management, and holistic approaches, we aim to treat the whole person. This integrated care model strengthens recovery outcomes and supports our clients in achieving long-lasting wellness and stability.
