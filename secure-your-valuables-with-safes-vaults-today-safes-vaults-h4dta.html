How do I maintain my safe or vault?
Maintaining your safe or vault is essential for ensuring it remains functional and secure over time. First, routinely inspect the locking mechanism and hinges for wear and tear. Use a cloth to wipe away dust and debris, particularly around electronic components. If your safe includes a moisture control system, ensure it is functional to prevent rust or spoilage of contents. Moreover, changing the battery in electronic locks periodically is necessary to avoid malfunctions. Contact North Country Armory for any professional maintenance advice or service if needed.
