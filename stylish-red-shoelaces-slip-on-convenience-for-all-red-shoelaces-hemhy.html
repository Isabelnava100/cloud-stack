How do I install Bread Elastic Shoelaces?
Installing Bread Elastic Shoelaces is a straightforward process. Begin by removing your old shoelaces. Then, take your new elastic shoelaces and thread them through the eyelets of your shoes, starting from the bottom. The elasticity allows for easier adjustments as you fit them to your desired tightness. Once they are threaded, make sure the aglets are secured, and you’re ready to enjoy the comfort and convenience of slip-on footwear!

How do I clean my Bread Elastic Shoelaces?
Cleaning your Bread Elastic Shoelaces is simple. Since they are made from durable polyester, you can easily clean them by wiping with a damp cloth. For deeper cleaning, you can hand wash them in mild soapy water and rinse thoroughly. Avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach, as these can damage the elastic material. Once cleaned, let them air dry, and they'll be ready for use again, ensuring that your red shoelaces stay looking fresh and vibrant!
