Frequently Asked Questions

Dallmann Confections Inc is a high-end chocolate business specializing in handcrafted chocolate experiences. Founded by Isabella Knack, a third-generation chocolatier from Austria, we merge traditional European techniques with modern artistry. Our confections are created using family recipes, ensuring that our chocolates maintain the rich heritage of old-world craftsmanship while integrating innovative designs to delight the senses.

A virtual chocolate tasting is a unique online event where participants can experience the exquisite flavors of handcrafted chocolates from the comfort of their homes. During the tasting, an expert guide leads participants through a curated selection of chocolates, sharing insights into their crafting process and flavor profiles. This interactive experience allows chocolate lovers to appreciate our European influences and the artistry behind each piece while enjoying it with friends, family, or colleagues, despite physical distances.

To participate in a virtual chocolate tasting with Dallmann Confections, you simply need to sign up for one of our scheduled sessions on our website. Once registered, you'll receive a tasting kit delivered to your door containing a selection of our handcrafted chocolates. On the tasting day, you’ll join a live session via video conferencing where Isabella Knack or one of our experts will guide you through an exploration of the chocolates, discussing flavors, textures, and the techniques used in creating these delightful confections.

A handcrafted chocolate experience with Dallmann Confections offers an indulgent journey through exquisite flavors and textures. You'll get the chance to taste a selection of our artisanal chocolates that reflect European traditions infused with artistic twists. Each piece is uniquely crafted, showcasing different flavor profiles that range from classic pralines to innovative combinations. Expect a deeper understanding of the craftsmanship involved in chocolate making, as well as personal stories behind each recipe that connect to our family's rich heritage.

How do I order for a virtual chocolate tasting event?
To order for a virtual chocolate tasting event, visit our website and browse the upcoming tasting events. Once you find an event that suits your schedule, click on the registration link to secure your spot. After registration, you’ll be prompted to provide shipping details for your tasting kit, which includes a carefully curated selection of our finest handcrafted chocolates. Make sure to register in advance to allow ample time for the shipping of your tasting kit before the event date.
